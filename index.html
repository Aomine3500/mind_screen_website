Why voice

Because it's easier to open up when you're heard

Replacing checkbox questionnaires with genuine dialogue lowers the barrier to honest self-reflection. The conversational format mirrors a therapeutic interaction — creating a sense of being heard, not simply measured — without compromising scientific rigor.
